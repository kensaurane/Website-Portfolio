About Me

Creative Artist

A consistently high-achieving honor student from elementary through college, I bring a strong academic background to my artistic pursuits. During my school years, I actively contributed to the school publication as an editorial cartoonist, showcasing my creativity and visual storytelling skills. My professional journey led me to work as a field videographer and editor for Banco Santiago de Libon, where I continue to hone my skills in visual storytelling and content creation. This experience provides me with valuable insights into the world of finance while allowing me to express my creativity through the lens. As a versatile artist, I am deeply involved in various forms of art, ranging from traditional to digital and 3D. My commitment extends to taking on art commissions, where I leverage my skills to bring the visions of clients to life. Through my diverse experiences and dedication to both academic and artistic excellence, I continue to explore new avenues in the world of visual arts.

My Services

Traditional Art

I accept traditional art commissions, specializing in the timeless elegance of ballpoint pen and pencil art. With expertise in these mediums, each commissioned piece is crafted with intricate details, resulting in a unique and personalized work of art.

Digital Art

Specializing in digital art, I excel in character design commissions. With a keen eye for detail and creativity, each digital creation reflects a unique and thoughtfully crafted character, bringing imaginative visions to life.

Graphic Design

Focused on graphic design, I've contributed to art convention projects and collaborated with international influencers. My designs combine creativity and strategic visuals for a lasting impact on digital platforms.

Photography/Videography

As a versatile artist, I excel in photography and videography, including editing, with experience contributing to a bank company. My visual storytelling expertise ensures impactful and polished content, blending creativity and corporate sophistication.

3d Modeling

Specializing in 3D modeling, I have a particular expertise in character modeling and hard surface modeling. Proficient in Maya and Blender, I bring digital creations to life with a blend of creativity and technical proficiency.

Animation

Proficient in animation, I specialize in digital, 3D, traditional, and stop-motion animation. With a versatile approach, I bring a wide range of characters and stories to life through dynamic and engaging visual storytelling.
